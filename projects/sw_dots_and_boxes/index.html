Dots and Boxes for Stormworks



A Dots and Boxes game made in Lua for Stormworks in an "Microcontroller". The challenge is that every lua component has a max of 4096 characters, including spaces, new lines and tabs. And multiple lua components can't communicate with eachother except with max 32 numbers and 32 booleans.

Link to source code because ingame it is minified to fit within the character limit.
